The ceremony and festivities will take place on September 17, 2017 at DeLille Cellars Chateau in Woodinville, Washington.

The venue is located on the east side of Woodinville-Redmond Rd NE, across the street from the Woodinville Lavender farm. If you are coming from I-405, take exit 20 by Totem Lake heading east on NE 124th St. After 2.3 miles, turn left/north on Woodinville-Redmond Rd NE. The venue is on the right after 1.2 miles.

See the map below for the exact location.

Event Timeline

The event will proceed at the following times:

  • 2 pm: Venue opens to guests.
  • 3 pm: Ceremony begins
  • 3:30 pm: End of ceremony, cocktail hour begins
  • 5 pm: Dinner
  • 7 pm: Post-dinner festivities
  • 10 pm: End of event
